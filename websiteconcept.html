Breakfast, made local.

Graze Breakfast Co. is a farm-forward morning kitchen on 50th Ave — eggs benedict done right, a drink menu people actually rave about, and a case of grab-and-go treats for when you're in a hurry.

See the menu Get directions
"Farm to table, said plainly — the food just tastes like where it came from."

Graze Breakfast Co. sits on 50th Ave as the newest addition to the Graze Restaurant Group family, alongside the Graze Food Truck and an upcoming Graze Burger Co. The kitchen leans on the same locally-sourced, farm-to-table approach the group has built its name on, just built around mornings.

Expect a room that fills up on weekends, a kitchen that takes gluten-free requests seriously (house-made GF bangers, a fryer currently kept GF), and a menu built around a few things done very well rather than forty things done fine.
