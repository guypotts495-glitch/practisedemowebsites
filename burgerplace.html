About the shop

Built on the burger, not the brand

Craving Burger Corner is a small, independently-run counter on 50th St — no drive-thru, no shortcuts, just burgers built from scratch and fries cut in-house. It's the kind of place that earns a 4.7-star rating the slow way: one order at a time.

Reviewers consistently point to the same three things — the price is fair for what you get, the shop is spotless, and the people behind the counter clearly care. That's the whole pitch.

  • 100% homemade patties, no shortcuts
  • Hand-cut fries and made-to-order poutine
  • Full menu of salads, wraps, and sandwiches too
  • Order online for pickup or curbside
  • Small shop, kept spotless, service called out by name in reviews
