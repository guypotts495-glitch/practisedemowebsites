"We don't just serve dessert — we serve a whole vibe."

Unicorn Cafe opened its doors on 50 Ave in Wetaskiwin as a small, pastel-drenched escape from the everyday — the kind of place where a Tuesday afternoon coffee run turns into an hour of catching up over towering milkshakes.

Everything on the menu is built to be shared and shown off: layered freakshakes loaded with candy and soft-serve, bubble tea in flavours that change with the season, rolled ice cream made to order, and rainbow waffles that taste as good as they photograph. It's a family-friendly spot first — sticky tables, birthday parties, and kids picking toppings included.
