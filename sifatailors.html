Sifa Tailors & Dry Clean is a locally and family owned shop, dedicated to alterations, repairs, and dry cleaning done the careful way. The shop handles everything from resizing a dress down several sizes to replacing a jacket zipper that's been dead for years — the kind of work that either gets done right or doesn't get done at all.

Dry cleaning and laundry are handled using environmentally friendly processes, and the shop's reputation has been built almost entirely on word of mouth — customers who tried several tailors before finding this one and stopped looking.
